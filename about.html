About

Hi, I'm Izzy — a social media manager, content creator and consultant with a passion for helping brands build a meaningful online presence.

After graduating with an exceptional First Class Honours degree in Advertising and Digital Marketing, I've combined creative thinking with strategic insight to help brands stand out in an increasingly competitive digital space. My journey in social media began in 2020, creating content for my own platforms, which gave me first-hand experience of what it takes to grow an engaged audience and create content that genuinely resonates.

Since then, I've worked on both sides of the industry. As a content creator, I've partnered with brands to produce authentic, engaging campaigns, and as a social media manager, I've developed content strategies, managed platforms and delivered marketing that supports long-term brand growth. Having experience from both perspectives allows me to understand what audiences connect with and what brands need to achieve real results.

What I enjoy most is taking a brand's vision and bringing it to life online. Whether it's refining a social strategy, creating compelling content or building a stronger digital presence, I'm passionate about helping businesses connect with their audience, grow with confidence and create a lasting impact.
